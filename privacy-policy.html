1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our tools, any text you type (topics, keywords, niches) is processed entirely in your browser. This data is not transmitted to or stored on our servers. If you contact us via our contact form or email, we collect the name, email address, and message content you provide.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit CreatorTools Hub, we automatically collect certain technical information including: your IP address (anonymized), browser type and version, operating system, pages visited and time spent on each, referring URLs, and general geographic location (country/region level).

This information is collected through analytics services and advertising platforms (see Section 4) and is used in aggregate form to understand how our tools are being used.

1.3 Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies. See Section 3 for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve CreatorTools Hub and its tools
  • Understand how visitors use the site so we can make data-driven product improvements
  • Serve relevant advertisements to support the free operation of the service
  • Respond to messages you send us via our contact form or email
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information to build individual user profiles, sell it to third parties, or send unsolicited marketing communications.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

CreatorTools Hub uses the following types of cookies:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly. They cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated data about how visitors use our site. This data helps us understand which tools are most used, where visitors come from, and how we can improve. Google Analytics data is anonymized ‚Äî we have enabled IP anonymization and do not use any personally identifiable information for analytics.

Advertising Cookies

We display advertisements through Google AdSense to fund the free operation of this service. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads relevant to your interests, based on your browsing activity across websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect how the site functions. For more information on managing cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.

5. Data Retention

We do not store the text inputs you enter into our tools. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 24 months to allow us to respond to follow-up enquiries and track product feedback trends, after which they are deleted. Anonymized analytics data is retained for up to 26 months as per Google Analytics's standard retention window.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your data for marketing or profiling
  • Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@creatortoolshub.com. We will respond within 30 days. Note that because we collect very limited personal data, many of these rights may have limited applicability.

EU/EEA residents have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We honor applicable rights for all users regardless of location.

7. Children's Privacy

CreatorTools Hub is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@creatortoolshub.com and we will take steps to delete that information.
